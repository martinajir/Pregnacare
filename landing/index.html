Our motivation

In developing areas, where internet access isn't commonplace, it may be hard to get access to quality healthcare. Especially during pregnancy, healthcare cannot be underestimated. Auntee aims to help with that.

Backed by women

Data from a study conducted on the topic of SMS messages show that pregnant women are interested in receiving text messages and phone calls with educational information about their pregnancy.

What does Auntee do?

Auntee is a friendly chatbot aiming to improve the way pregnant women in remote areas of the world interact with healthcare professionals. First, Auntee asks for basic information regarding the woman's diet, activity levels and possible genetic illnesses. Based on that information, Auntee gives advice and reminds women at certain point of their pregnancy to take action, go for bloodtests or ultrasounds.

How can I sign up for Auntee?

Right now, Auntee is still under development. In the future, we aim to let women sign up by sending a simple text message to a phone number. From then on, Auntee will take care of the rest. Ideally, we would like to market Auntee by collaborating with health providers so that Auntee would be offered as a pregnancy companion right when the woman first finds out she is pregnant.
